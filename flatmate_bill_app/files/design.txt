Title: Flatmates Bill

Description:
An app that gets bill amount as input and No. Of days each flatmate stayed in the house for mentioned period.
returns how much each flatmate have to pay.
It also generates a PDF report with flatmate name, bill period and amount need to be paid by each.

Objects:
Bill -> amount, period
Flatmate -> name, days_stayed, pays(bill),
PdfReport -> filename, generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
