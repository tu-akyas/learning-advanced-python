Title: Calories Calculator
Description: This is an application that gets the weight, height, age, city and country
 of the user as input and then scrapes the temperature of the user's location and calculates
 the amount of calories that user will need.
 Objects:
    Calorie:
        - Weight
        - Height
        - Age
        - temperature
        - calculate()
    Temperature:
        - Country
        - City
        - get()
